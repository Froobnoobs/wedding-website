How we met

Like all good covid love stories
we met online and then in person by skirting Sydney's social distancing rules of course!

Having a first date in small park outside of Chloe's work, and going to a so called "beach" in liverpool, to hunt for "coconuts", since that was best you could do back then.

Since nothing was open, a woolies run was the best we could do for some choccies and sparkling water/kombucha
( because gotta be somewhat healthy on a first date )



Since then, our mutual love for just being in nature really drew us together

Sometimes for good



And sometimes for the worst...



But through the ups and downs, we've achieved many a great thing in our relatively short time, so here we are!
We can't wait to share this day with you, as small as it might be, as all of you have made us possible in various ways ❤️
